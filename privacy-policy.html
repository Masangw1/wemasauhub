Privacy Policy for Savings Group Contributions Add-On Last Updated: [19 Feb, 2025] This Privacy Policy explains how the Savings Group Contributions Add-On (the "Add-On") collects, uses, and protects your information. This Add-On is designed to assist in managing contributions for your savings group by sending reminder and thank-you emails to members based on their payment status. By using this Add-On, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Collect The Add-On processes the following information: Member Names and Email Addresses: These are used to send reminder and thank-you emails. Contribution Dates and Payment Status: This information is used to determine whether a reminder or thank-you email should be sent. Spreadsheet Data: The Add-On accesses data within your Google Sheets spreadsheet to perform its functions. All data processed by the Add-On is stored solely within your Google Sheets spreadsheet and is not collected, stored, or shared with any third parties. 2. How We Use Your Information The Add-On uses the information collected for the following purposes: To send reminder emails to members who have not yet made their contributions. To send thank-you emails to members who have completed their contributions. To perform date-based checks and automate email notifications as per your settings. 3. Data Storage and Security All data processed by the Add-On is stored within your Google Sheets spreadsheet and is subject to Google's security and privacy policies. The Add-On does not store, transmit, or share any data with external servers or third parties. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your Google account and spreadsheet. 4. Permissions Required To function properly, the Add-On requires the following permissions: Read access to your Google Sheets spreadsheet: To access contribution data and payment status. Send emails on your behalf: To send reminder and thank-you emails to members. These permissions are used solely for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and are not abused or misused in any way. 5. Your Rights You have full control over the data processed by the Add-On. You can revoke the Add-On's access to your Google account at any time by removing it from your Google Workspace or account settings. You can modify or delete the data in your Google Sheets spreadsheet at any time. 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. 7.
